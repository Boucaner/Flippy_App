How to Play:

1. Click on any tile to flip it and reveal its color.
2. Click on another tile to find a matching color.
3. If the colors match, both tiles will be removed from the board.
4. If the colors don't match, both tiles will flip back over.
5. Try to remove all tiles with the fewest flips possible!
6. The game ends when all tiles have been matched and removed.
